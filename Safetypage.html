  • Safety Information

    • Fire Hazards

    Cigarettes are banned for use on all buses, due to fire and smoke hazards. Please refrain from using them while on the bus. Vapes are also banned, to reduce air pollution.

    Emergency Exit

    An Emergency exit is stationed in every bus, halfway between the front and the back of the bus. In case of an emergency, make sure to exit through the emergency exit calmly, in a single file line.

    Seatbelts

    Please keep your seatbelts on for the duration of your trip. A majority of road fatalities are caused by seatbelts not being in use. Seatbelts are found to the left of every seat, and are regulalry checked on and undergo maintenance when needed to prevent accidents.
